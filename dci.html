in 2019 i was a participant in drum corps international, a league similar to the NFL but for competitive marching ensembles. I traveled the country with genesis and performed a 12 minute show entitled "from the ground up." more information can be found about DCI here. you can find a more detailed explanation of genesis here.
expanding more on my tours with genesis-
in the summer of 2019 i set aside all other activities and free time to devote my summer to working 14 hours every day for around 80 days towards this one show. This activity is comparable to high school marching band because of the rigorous practice and performance schedules, but the athleticism required for dci is similar to that of highly intensive sports. I travelled the country along with 150 other members of the group who eventually became my family. We slept on high school gym floors and in busses traveling from west to east coast. We would perform in large NFL stadiums and high school football fields. This has been the best experience of my life and i have grown mentally and physically from it.
