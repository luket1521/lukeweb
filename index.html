About Me:

Hi! I'm Luke Taylor. I am currently in the junior class of Lake Travis High School, and I plan to graduate in the spring semester of 2021. Some of my passions include music, particularly classical euphonium and jazz, computer science, and robotics. I also have a deep commitment to service, and have accumulated over 500 service hours. I have also received the US congressional Gold medal in 2020. In college, I plan to study computer science within the state of Texas and continue to explore my love for music as well.
