i was a part of the lake travis high school band program for all four years of high school. I used to play the Euphonium in concert season, the baritone during the marching season, and the bass trombone in jazz band. While i participated in the program, the band performed around the country, including St. Louis, New York, and many other local events.
For me, band used to include marching season, concert season, and jazz band. This would take hours out of every day and total up to 20-30 hours some weeks. I have marched in 4 of our productions, entitled Junkestra, Project Stravinsky, Eternal, and March Fourth. Links to each can be found below. During concert season, I have performed works by maslanka, grainger, many others and also prepared solo and chamber ensembles every year. In jazz band we have a wide selection of music, ranging from classic jazz to modern funk. The ensemble would perform for the community at events in nursing homes to concert halls. In all areas of band we consider it our privilege to bring more music and artistry to our community through performance and hard work.
